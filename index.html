WHAT and WHY

For many years I have asked myself the questions, why still today, so many corporates, do not understand the Power of Company Culture, the Agile Manifesto, the Motivational Factors? Why do they still believe it is Process first, Technology Second, and People last?

Doing my first website in 1998, which probably looked like spaghetti, I found my destiny in life, to create awesome products, that are state of the art from all angles, including ACCESSIBILITY.

Today, in 2022, I am defined as a "Digital Expert", I have lectured for big digital companies such as Red Hat, have been doing Design Systems, before it even became a term. Had the privilege to meet up with Brad Frost in the UK, for a DS event, and have interacted through the Meetup, Events I have been part of, with some of the most skilled and passionate people within Design Systems.

24 years now and I still LOVE the craft, now even more than before, that Web Accessibility is actually becoming the default, as it should have always been.

For me a Design System represents so much more than just Components, Design Principles, and Technology, doing it right, it can be the glue that can get the digital tribe to form an astonishing DIGITAL CULTURE, the book I am realizing this year in Silicon Valley for the SVLLS event.

I have written so much about Design Systems in my coming book The Digital Culture, that it has become its own life, therefore I and my writing team has decided that this will become its own project, a book, first of all, where my community will have the opportunity to share their "Design Systems and beyond" stories.

People I talked to about the concept UserSystem know that it could also be a product, but first things first, that will be a future project, let us first finish the book, because it is missing.

Join me, on our first open mic event, to kick off the project, join the group on LinkedIn.
